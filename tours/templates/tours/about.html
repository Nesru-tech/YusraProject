About Yusra Tour & Travel

We provide Umrah, health, and vacation travel services with a heart for excellence.

Who We Are

Based in Ethiopia, Yusra Tour and Travel is a leading agency that designs trips for Umrah, health care, and fun getaways. Our friendly team works hard to blend expert planning with a personal touch, ensuring every journey feels smooth and meaningful.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide travelers with expertly planned, personalized, and enriching journeys that exceed expectations. We are committed to delivering outstanding customer service, promoting responsible tourism, and creating lasting memories for every client—whether they're exploring their own country or venturing to the far corners of the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading travel agency known for crafting unique, meaningful, and seamless travel experiences. We aim to connect travelers with the beauty and diversity of the world, fostering cultural understanding, environmental responsibility, and personal growth through every journey we plan.

Our Core Services

Umrah Packages

We provide budget-friendly Umrah packages, each one carefully organized and led by knowledgeable guides who support you every step of the way.

Health Tourism

We team up with leading hospitals to create travel packages centred on your health.

Leisure & Vacation

Discover relaxing getaways and unforgettable holiday destinations around the world.
